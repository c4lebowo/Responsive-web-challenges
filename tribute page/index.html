Chalino Sanchez

Life and death of the king of the corrido

Chalino Sanchez with his mamalona.

Here's a time line of Chalino Sanchez life:

  • 1960 - Born in Badiraguato, Sinaloa
  • 1975 - His sister is kidnapped by "Chapo Perez", a rich and dangerous man.
  • 1977 - A 17-year-old Chalino saw the man in a party and alledgedly shot him in revenge, killing him. After this act he left Tijuana, leaving his work as an immigrant smuggler
  • The same year - Crossed into the United States as an undocumented migrant worker. He began in Oregon and later he moved to Los Angeles to live with his aunt in Inglewood, California, washing dishes, selling cars and dealt small quantities of marijuana and cocaine.
  • 1984 - Got married with his cousin Rosalba, having two kids, Adán Sanchez and Cynthia Sanchez
  • 1984 - His brother, Armando Sanchez was shot and killed in a hotel in Tijuana, composing his first corrido (or ballad) about him. Around this time, Chalino was arrested. He began to compose song about his fellow immates and later he began earning money and would be gifted with guns and 'presents' by his customers
  • 1989 - Chalino recorded his first cassette with 15 songs, selling it in many businesses across South Central Los Angeles
  • 1992 - Chalino had an exchange of shots while performing at the Los Arcos night club in the desert city of Coachella, he was hospitalized in critical condition and underwent surgery at Desert Hospital, Palm springs
  • 1992 - four months after the incident, Chalino performed at the Salon Bugambillas, Cuiiacán. After midnight, Chalino drove away from the club with two of his brothers, a cousin, and several young women. They were pulled over by a group of armed men in Chevrolet Suburbans. They showed state police ID cards and told Chalino their commandant wanted to see him. Chalino agreed and got into one of their cars while the others followed behind. His body was found in an irrigation canal near highway 15.
